02 THE PROBLEM

Research is expensive.
Discipline is harder.

01

Too much signal

Markets move faster than a person can collect, compare, and contextualize every relevant source.

02

Too little structure

A strategy is only useful when every decision is checked against the same rules, limits, and intent.

03

Wrong automation

Black-box execution removes the most important control: the investor’s final decision.

Your strategy,
amplified by AI.

Throttle converts investment intent into a repeatable research-and-approval loop.

  1. 01
    ⌁

    Policy

    Set valuation caps, position limits, sectors, risk constraints, and the rules that define a valid move.

    YOU DEFINE THE SYSTEM
  2. 02
    ✳

    Morning

    A hosted agent researches the market against your policy and filters noise before it reaches you.

    THE AGENT DOES THE WORK
  3. 03
    ≡

    Every proposed move arrives with a concise explanation and receipts tied directly to your rules.

    EVERY MOVE EXPLAINED
  4. 04

    YOUR DECISION IS THE GATE
