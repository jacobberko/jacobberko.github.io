Album: Waves


Driven by a deep passion for music, I undertook the challenge of self-teaching music production through extensive tutorials and hands-on experimentation. My dedication to mastering this skill has culminated in the creation of my first instrumental, upbeat album, titled "Waves." Out on Apple Music and Spotify October 25, 2024.


Novel: The Cure


In a world gripped by a devastating pandemic, one brilliant scientist emerges as humanity's savior, discovering the cure that offers hope to millions. As nations scramble to secure the life-saving treatment, the scientist's influence grows, elevating him to unparalleled heights of power and prestige. But behind the veil of his success lies a dark secret that could shatter the foundation of his legacy. "The Cure" is a gripping tale of ambition, deception, and the unforeseen consequences of a single man's quest for dominance.
