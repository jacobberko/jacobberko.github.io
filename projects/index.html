Projects


The Game of Life

The Game of Life is a project that was coded in functional programming language, OCaml. The game occurs in the terminal and can be played with one player or two. It is themed around Cornell.


Satisfy

"Satisfy" is a minimalist puzzle game that delights in offering simple yet satisfying challenges. Each level tasks players with arranging elements or connecting dots to create a sense of order and balance. With its clean graphics and intuitive gameplay, "Satisfy" rewards players with the serene satisfaction of seeing each puzzle click into place, providing a calm, meditative experience as they progress through increasingly creative and complex stages.
