Projects


The Game of Life

The Game of Life is a project that was coded in functional programming language, OCaml. The game occurs in the terminal and can be played with one player or two. It is themed around Cornell.


Satisfy

Satisfy is a game that revolves around simplicity and satisfaction in the little things in life. The goal of the game is to solve puzzles that lead to satisfying endings, like putting the last piece of a puzzle in, or organizing a cluttered bedroom.
